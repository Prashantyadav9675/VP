CET Group C Examination 2025 | Advt No. 01/2025

HSSC CET Group C Exam 2025 Notification Released

Apply Online from 28/05/2025 to 12/06/2025

Overview

Haryana Staff Selection Commission (HSSC) has released the Common Eligibility Test (CET) notification which will be necessary for upcoming Group C recruitments in Haryana. Candidates from Haryana or other states who wish to join Group C services can apply online from 28 May 2025 to 12 June 2025.

Important Dates

Event Date
Application Begin 28/05/2025
Last Date for Apply Online 12/06/2025
Complete Form Last Date 14/06/2025
Exam Date As per Schedule
Admit Card Available Before Exam

Application Fee

Category Fee
Male Candidates (General/Other State) ₹500/-
Male Candidates (Haryana Reserve Category) ₹250/-
Female Candidates (General/EWS - Haryana) ₹250/-
Female Candidates (Haryana Reserve Category) ₹250/-

Payment Mode: Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking / UPI Only

Eligibility Criteria

Age Limit (as on 12/06/2025)

Category Age Range
Minimum Age 18 Years
Maximum Age 42 Years

Age relaxation applicable as per HSSC Group C Recruitment Rules

Educational Qualification

  • 10+2 Intermediate Exam Passed from Any Recognized Board in India

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official HSSC website
  2. Read the complete notification carefully before applying
  3. Prepare all required documents:
    • Educational certificates
    • ID proof
    • Address proof
    • Recent photograph (passport size)
    • Signature scan
  4. Fill the online application form carefully
  5. Upload scanned documents as per specifications
  6. Pay the application fee (if applicable)
  7. Submit the form and take printout for future reference
